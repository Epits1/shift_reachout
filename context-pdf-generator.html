What Is Infobip Shift?

Infobip Shift is not a traditional developer conference.

It is a curated platform for engineering leadership and product decision-makers — where companies building at scale share real challenges, trade-offs, and lessons learned.

Over the past 15 years, Shift has evolved into a destination where:

  • CTOs, CIOs, and engineering leaders shape industry conversations
  • Teams building real systems share firsthand experience — not theory
  • Companies position themselves as builders, not commentators

Shift sits at the intersection of:

technical depth × executive audience × real-world application

Why Companies Partner

Unlike traditional conferences, Infobip Shift serves three distinct strategic purposes:

✓ Employer Branding

Direct access to senior engineers and technical leaders through technical workshops, branded stages, expo activations, and curated networking. Position your company as engineering-driven and build long-term credibility within the developer ecosystem.

✓ Product & Market Positioning

Launch or reinforce products in a high-engagement environment through stage presence, sponsored technical deep dives, livestream branding, and premium expo activations. Enable product storytelling through interaction and authority, not passive visibility.

✓ Client & Executive Engagement

Leverage Infobip Shift as a curated experience platform for your own clients and strategic accounts. The multi-day format and Mediterranean setting allow you to host key clients in an environment blending technical depth, networking, and premium experience.

Experience & Format

Infobip Shift is structured as a multi-day, in-person technology event with parallel stages, expo space, and dedicated networking zones in Zadar, Croatia.

For partners, the format enables:
  • Multi-day exposure rather than single-session visibility
  • Structured and informal interaction with 4,000+ attendees
  • Client hosting opportunities outside the main program
  • Integration across stages, expo, and networking
  • Extended engagement beyond scheduled talks

The Setting Advantage

The combination of technical content and destination setting consistently results in higher partner dwell time and extended informal engagement compared to single-day conference formats. Zadar's Mediterranean setting creates a premium atmosphere that facilitates deeper relationships.
