Strategic Positioning

Infobip Shift is Infobip's strategic credibility platform in the global technology ecosystem — not just a conference, but a brand and relationship accelerator.

It has evolved from a developer conference into a destination where:

  • Senior engineering and technology leadership gather on real challenges
  • CTOs, CIOs, and heads of engineering from global companies influence conversations
  • Companies actively shaping technology (not just following trends) position themselves as innovators

Why Companies Partner

✓ Employer Branding

Direct access to senior engineers and technical leaders. Companies position themselves as engineering-driven and build long-term credibility within the developer ecosystem.

✓ Product & Market Positioning

Launch or reinforce products through stage presence, sponsored technical deep dives, livestream branding, and premium expo activations. Product storytelling through interaction and authority, not passive visibility.

✓ Client & Executive Engagement

Leverage Shift as a curated experience platform for your own clients and strategic accounts. The multi-day format and Mediterranean setting enable deeper relationships.

Experience & Format

Multi-day, in-person technology event with parallel stages, expo space, and networking zones. The setting and multi-day structure result in higher engagement and extended informal connection.

For partners:
  • ✓ Multi-day exposure vs. single-session visibility
  • ✓ Structured and informal interaction with attendees
  • ✓ Client hosting opportunities outside the main program
  • ✓ Integration across stages, expo, and networking
  • ✓ Extended engagement beyond scheduled talks
